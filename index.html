Computational Political Science • Penn State

Methodologist studying conflict, security, and political behavior through data.

I build research pipelines that connect machine learning, network science, and text analysis to high-stakes political questions. My work emphasizes transparent measurement, reproducible workflows, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Across projects, I examine conflict dynamics, information environments, and institutions that shape violence, governance, and collective behavior.
